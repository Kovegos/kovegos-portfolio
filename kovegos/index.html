About Kovegos

I got into this
because of cinema.

Not the industry — the feeling. That thing that happens when an image, a sound, and a moment align and something shifts inside you. I've been chasing that since I was a kid, and I found my way to it through motion and 3D.

I work under the name Kovegos. Based in Buenos Aires, with over three years of experience delivering visual content for brands across 30+ countries. I've worked with studios and clients in the US, Europe, and Australia — from e-commerce product animation to branded campaigns for companies like Prime Video, Sancor Seguros, Jumbo, and Prisma.

What drives me isn't the tool or the technique — it's whether the final piece makes you feel something. Empty visuals don't interest me. I want the work to land.

I hold Italian citizenship and I'm open to remote work or relocation.
